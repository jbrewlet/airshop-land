SHOPBOARD DOWNLOAD LOGIN

Pair your Shopboard

Pairing links your AirShop account tier to a Shopboard on your network. Codes are one-time and expire in 15 minutes.

Where this lives in the product

When you use the main AirShop app (after sign-in), open Settings → Integrations → Shopboard to generate a code and manage downloads. That experience runs on airshop.work — this page is a fallback / operator form for teams wiring the integration before the in-app screen ships.

Security: The mint secret is equivalent to a password. Do not store it in the browser, share it in screenshots, or commit it to git. Use only over HTTPS.

Generate a pairing code

Enter the operator mint secret, how the shop should appear on the Pi, and submit. You will see a code to type on the Shopboard first-boot wizard or in Admin → AirShop → Pair.
