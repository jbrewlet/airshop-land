Reorder Queue alerts and a daily digest

AirShop now watches your stock for you. Every item carries a reorder status, the Reorder Queue gives you a ranked shortlist of what needs attention, and a daily digest email keeps the right people ahead of stockouts.

Running low used to mean noticing too late. Now AirShop tracks each item's reorder status, surfaces what needs ordering, and can email a daily summary so nothing slips through.

Every item has a reorder status

Instead of reading raw quantities, each item shows where it stands:

  • Order Now — out of stock or at/below threshold. Order today.
  • Order Soon — running low. Get ahead of it.
  • On Order — a purchase order is already placed, so you don't double-order.
  • Stocked — healthy. No action needed.

The Reorder Queue is your daily shortlist

The Reorder Queue filters Inventory Items to everything needing attention, ranked so the most urgent rises to the top, with quantity and threshold in view and newly flagged items highlighted. Place an order, mark the item On Order, and it drops off the urgent list.

Alerts that come to you

You shouldn't have to remember to check. AirShop pushes reorder awareness two ways:

  • In-app change notice — when you open AirShop, a notice summarizes inventory changes since your last visit, with Order Now and Order Soon items surfaced first.
  • Daily reorder digest email — a clean summary of items that need attention, grouped by status with quantity and threshold, sent once a day to the people you choose. If nothing needs attention, no email goes out.

You control who hears about what

Everything lives in Settings → Inventory Settings. Admins set organization defaults — toggle the in-app notice and digest, choose which statuses count, and pick the recipients. Each person can then fine-tune their own alerts and even override which statuses appear in their personal digest. A Send test button previews exactly what recipients will get.
