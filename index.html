Powerful Features for Modern Business

We're constantly adding new tools to help you grow your business.

Quoting

  • Streamline Inquiry to Quote

    Take inquiries right on your website with our embedded form. No double entry or copying between apps.

  • Know When Quotes are Viewed

    Our quotes live track when customers view your quotes.

  • Beautiful Quote Builder

    Build quotes from your inventory with images and professional formatting.

  • Custom One-Off Items

    Create custom items right in your quote without adding them to inventory.

  • Smart Composite Items

    Build out detailed line item assemblies while keeping customer-facing quotes clean and clear.

  • Integrated Payments

    Take partial or full payments instantly after quote acceptance with built-in Stripe integration.

  • Margin Tracking

    Know your margins on each item before you send.

Inventory

  • Smart Ordering

    Keep on top of what needs to be ordered so you don't get blocked by lack of inventory.

  • Quick Search Codes ✨

    Find items fast with unique short codes that you can search by and refer to.

  • Bulk Import

    Moving to a new system sucks, we've built a quick bulk import tool to make that easy.

  • Kanban Cards & Labels

    Print physical cards & labels in multiple sizes to trigger reorders and manage inventory visually.

  • QR Code Integration

    Automatically integrated QR codes for easy barcode or phone camera scanning.

  • Seamless Quote Integration

    No more copying data between spreadsheets or from your Amazon orders. Use your items directly in your quotes.

Customer Management

  • Organization Overview

  • Integrated Communication

    Keep track of your customer's quote history and preferences.

Intelligence
